The main areas of disease manifestations

  • Impaired learning: Difficulty retaining new information
  • Word-finding problems: Struggling to recall the correct words, substituting them with others
  • Changes in communication: Shifts in facial expressions and difficulty expressing oneself clearly
  • Disrupted thought flow: Incomplete sentences and thoughts
  • Repetitive speech: Frequent repetition of words or phrases
  • Disorientation: Difficulty with time and place awareness
