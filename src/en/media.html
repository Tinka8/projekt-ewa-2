Media

Several Slovakian media outlets, including magazines like Život, Pravda, Bedeker zdravia, and radio stations like Rádio Regina and Rádio Slovensko, as well as TV TA3 and some internet portals, helped recruit participants for the research project. They disseminated information to the general public about the project and the opportunity to be involved.
