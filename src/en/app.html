Phone apps of the EWA project

App for the public

You can download the app for Android OS here

The test consists of naming and describing the images that are shown to the user on the mobile device. Upon completion, users will receive a notification with their results.

The app offers the possibility to find out the current state of the user's cognitive abilities and also to engage in longer-term monitoring.

App for IOS

The iOS app is available upon request. Please contact AXON PRO through their contact form .

App for research purposes

As part of this research, we developed an application to capture both anamnestic data and speech recordings. This user-friendly tool allows you to easily enter all the information required for the study:

  • Visual acuity test
  • Basic anamnestic questionnaire
  • MoCA test (Montreal Cognitive Assessment)
  • Other questionnaires (WAIS, GAD7, BDI...)
  • Voice recordings (naming and describing pictures)
